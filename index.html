About Our school

All SOULS HIGH, a world class school of first choice opened its doors to the public on 1 January 1950. It is registered with the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education as a formal school. This state of the art, cathlic church owned school is located in Mutoko. ASH is offering ZIMSEC and Advanced level ZIMSEC examinations to its learners. The school has also engaged staff with extensive ZIMSEC teaching experience in an effort to augment the envisaged vision. The curriculum which has a stint towards the sciences also has the following other subjects; Commercials, Humanities, Information and Communication Technology, Fine Arts, Sports and Cultural activities. The school has world class and state of the art I.T and Science Laboratories spacious classrooms, boarding and kitchen facilities that are virtually second to none. The mandate of the school is to inculcate in students, the expertise, exposure, skills and knowledge necessary to confront unusual challenges in the world and also to produce a new breed of scholars with 21st century competencies. ASH is a unique Christian school with an oriented value system designed to produce responsible and disciplined students who are morally upright, confident and fully endowed with competencies and qualities expected of an enlightened global citizen befitting future leadership and acceptance at any tertiary institution.
